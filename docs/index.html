About

Project

  • Teams will come up with projects related to the theme SmartCampus in NTU.
  • Teams are encouraged to not reuse any projects and build something new!
  • Projects will be judged based on Creativeness & Impressiveness, Usefulness, Technical Difficulties, Business Potential, and Theme Relevance.
  • Hardware hacks are welcome!

Eligibility

  • Students from Nanyang Technological University in REP, SCSE, EEE or other relevant schools
  • Admission is free for everyone and meals will be provided
  • Participants will form teams of at most 4 members.
  • Only selected participants will be notified of acceptance to the Hackathon.
